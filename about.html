Frequently Asked Questions

SEO & Backlinks

How quickly will I see SEO results from backlinks?

SEO improvements typically begin showing within 2-4 weeks as search engines crawl and index your press release. However, the full impact on rankings usually develops over 2-3 months. The permanence of our backlinks means they continue providing value indefinitely.

What makes a backlink "high quality"?

High-quality backlinks have several characteristics: they're dofollow (pass SEO value), come from relevant content, use descriptive anchor text, originate from a trusted domain, and are contextually placed. PressWire.ie backlinks tick all these boxes, especially for Irish businesses seeking local SEO improvement.

Can I include multiple backlinks in my press release?

Yes! We encourage it. You can include your main website, specific product pages, blog posts, and social media profiles. Each link provides SEO value and potential traffic. We recommend 3-5 strategic links for optimal impact without appearing spammy.

Do the backlinks remain permanent?

Yes, all press releases and their backlinks remain permanently published and indexed. This provides ongoing SEO value long after publication. Even if you unpublish a PR, search engines will have already indexed the links.

Platform & Process

Why is domain verification important?

Domain verification ensures only legitimate businesses can publish, maintaining the quality and trustworthiness of our platform. This exclusivity actually increases the SEO value of backlinks from PressWire.ie, as search engines trust verified sources more.

How fast is "60-second publishing"?

Once you complete payment, your press release goes live immediately and is accessible via its permanent URL. Search engine indexing typically begins within hours, and full indexing usually completes within 24-48 hours.

Can I edit my press release after publishing?

Yes, you have a 24-hour window to make edits after publication. This includes updating content, links, and contact information. After 24 hours, the PR becomes permanent to maintain integrity and SEO value.

What happens to my data?

We follow GDPR guidelines strictly. Your press release is public, but personal data is encrypted and never shared. We don't sell email lists or contact information. You can request data deletion at any time (though published PRs remain for SEO continuity).
